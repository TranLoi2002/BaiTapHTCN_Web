One particular book which is recommended reading is The Stress Lawyer by Jonh Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occirs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the coursts. The Street Lawyer is a great book.It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client
